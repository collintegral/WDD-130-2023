The Trouble with Kobolds

Description: A party of rookie adventurers has taken on more than they bargained for as they delve deeper than they ever thought possible, striving to uncover the secrets long-buried beneath sleepy Okkolm's sewers. The deeper they go, the more the question rises: why are there so many dang kobolds down here?

This campaign emphasizes dungeon-delving, combat, and relationships with the town of Okkolm. The party is currently level 3.

Players:
  • Keith Ridgeward, Halfling Champion
  • Luke E. Chot, Kobold Gunslinger
  • Mira Drue, Human Wizard
  • Lizzie, Lizardfolk Oracle

    • The Dragonsbane Five

    Description: In the quiet port town of Otari, a group of ragtag adventurers managed to best a young dragon that was threating the fishing enterprise. Now, they're forced to deal with the unexpected fame - and responsibility - that comes with slaying a dragon. Undead threats, devil-summoning spies, and annoyingly persistent fey; are they connected, or is their homeland simply more full of trouble than they had ever realized? They'll deal with it all the same, to spread the reputation of the Dragonsbane Five!

    This campaign emphasizes party dynamics, combat, and a hint of mystery. The party is currently level 6.

    Players:
  • Heath Kumoso, Half-Elf Barbarian
  • Jack Starborne, Nephilim Champion
  • Kryioc, Gnome Sorcerer
  • Erhu Savoir, Elf Bard
  • Zarth, Catfolk Monk

    • Here Be Trees

    Description: Far from the bustling metropolises on the Eastern Empire's shores lies a dense, poorly-mapped jungle, filled with danger, mystery, and hopefully, treasure! Three brave explorers set out to make a name for themselves charting the unexplored lands and taming their most dangerous secrets.

    This campaign emphasizes exploration, survival, and open-ended adventure. The party is currently level 5.

    Players:
  • Elisha Duwaim, Half-Elf Fighter
  • Oshkayr, Fetchling Summoner
  • Thom, Dwarf Cleric

    • Highland Heroes of Yore

    Description: Long ago, mighty heroes of legend walked the land, defeating evil and saving the highlands from an invading army. Now, centuries later, a new party of adventurers has arisen to follow in their footsteps. Each has their own reason to trace the path of those ancient heroes. Will they find what they each seek? Or will they only find an early grave?

    This campaign emphasizes storytelling, heroic deeds, and larger-than-life personalities. The party is currently level 8.

    Players:
  • Urnwald the Imp, Halfling Rogue
  • Amaryllis Ashe, Human Witch
  • Jules, Goblin Ranger
  • Oriel, Human Bard
  • Tawana Vildeter, Elf Alchemist

    • The Seabreeze Ventures

    Description: Take to the high seas! The Seabreeze Venture is a majestic sailing ship. Or it was, years ago. Nowadays it creaks, leaks, and begs for a new crew. Thankfully, it's got it, in the form of a set of swashbuckling heroes and vagabonds all looking to make their fortune and tell a new story of daring deeds and grand adventure. That is, if they can keep their new boat afloat!

    This campaign emphasizes seafaring themes, tactical combat, and exploration. The party is currently level 2.

    Players:
